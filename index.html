Call for Contributions

Please consider submitting your work for presentation at NEHWS 2023. Submission link will be added soon.

Call for Sponsors

Sponsors of NEHWS will gain visibility for their companies and associate themselves with an active regional community that contributes to training the next-generation workforce in hardware security. NEHWS offers benefits according to the sponsorship level. Please contact the NEHWS organizers at contact@nehws.org to discuss existing ways in which you can introduce your company to the audience and contribute to the success of the workshop.
